AEO FAQ

Smart Lock Buyer Questions

What smart lock products does BOLVRA manufacture?

BOLVRA manufactures fingerprint smart locks, Tuya and TTLock app locks, face recognition locks, glass door locks, hotel locks and access control products for distributors, installers and project buyers.

Can BOLVRA support OEM and ODM customization?

Yes. We support logo, packaging, app configuration, lock structure, unlock methods, mortise matching and project-specific function combinations for private-label customers.

Which unlock methods are available?

Depending on the model, BOLVRA smart locks can support fingerprint, passcode, IC card, Tuya or TTLock app, face recognition and mechanical key backup.

How do buyers choose the right lock body?

For project orders, buyers should confirm door material, door thickness, opening direction, mortise size and required unlock methods before selecting a smart lock model.
