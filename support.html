Support

Practical support for distributors, installers and project buyers.

Support Built Around Real Projects

BOLVRA supports customers before, during and after an order. Our focus is practical: helping distributors choose the right range, helping installers confirm compatibility, and helping project buyers keep delivery and installation moving.

Pre-Sales Support

  • Product selection based on door type, application and target market.
  • Model matching for residential, rental, hotel, office and project use.
  • OEM / ODM discussions for branding, packaging, functions and project requirements.
  • Quotation, product data and order documentation for B2B purchasing.

Installation & Technical Support

  • Installation guidance, product manuals and available technical documents.
  • Remote troubleshooting for product setup, hardware and supported smart functions.
  • Project coordination for model confirmation, sample evaluation and batch orders.

After-Sales Support

For order-related questions, please provide your order number, model number, photos or video of the issue, and a clear description. This helps us assess the situation faster and provide the next step.

Support response time may vary by time zone, technical complexity and project size. Order-specific terms always follow the quotation, proforma invoice, sales contract or written order confirmation.
