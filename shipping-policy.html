Shipping Policy

Flexible shipping support for samples, bulk orders and international projects.

Shipping for Growing Global Markets

BOLVRA ships samples, bulk orders and project goods to international markets. The final shipping method is selected according to destination, order volume, urgency, product type and the commercial terms agreed with the buyer.

Available Shipping Methods

  • Express courier for samples and urgent small shipments.
  • Air freight for time-sensitive commercial orders.
  • Sea freight for bulk orders and project supply.
  • Other logistics solutions when confirmed in writing for the order.

Lead Time & Delivery Estimates

Production and shipping timelines are estimates until the order details, payment, customization requirements and logistics plan are confirmed. Delays caused by customs, carriers, weather, holidays, inspections or other events outside reasonable control may affect delivery.

Freight, Duties & Import Costs

Freight responsibility, insurance, customs clearance, duties and taxes follow the agreed quotation and shipping terms. Buyers should confirm local import requirements before shipment unless BOLVRA has specifically agreed to provide that service.

Inspection on Arrival

Please inspect cartons and goods promptly after delivery. If there is visible transport damage or a quantity discrepancy, keep the packaging and contact us with photos, videos and delivery documents as soon as possible.

The Incoterm, carrier, freight cost, transfer of risk and delivery responsibility stated in the order documents take priority over this general shipping policy.
