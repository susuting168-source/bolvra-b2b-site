# Asset Replacement Guide - V6

## Processed image folders

### `assets/images/branded/`
Factory and QC photos used in the website were processed for BOLVRA presentation. Obvious old-brand marks were replaced or visually neutralized where practical.

### `assets/images/optimized/`
Product images were processed from the original white-background files and reused in darker, more premium product sections.

## Replacing certificate images
The certificate matrix uses individual files:

`assets/images/certificates/certificate-01.webp` through `certificate-18.webp`

Replace these files with your real certificate scans or photos, keeping the same filenames. Recommended aspect ratio: vertical document, about 13:18.
